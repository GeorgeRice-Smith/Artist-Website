About

George Rice-Smith is an Artist and Art Technician with Administrative and Supervisory experience through work at The Royal Drawing School and for numerous artists and art galleries.  Born 1988. Based in London and Hampshire.

Dates Education & Qualifications
2013 Richard Ford Award, Royal Academy of Art; a travelling scholarship for figurative artists to study at Museo del Prado, Madrid
2008 – 2011 BA (Honours) Art & Visual Culture (First Class), University of the West of England, Bristol
​2007 – 2008 Foundation Diploma in Art & Design (Distinction). Alton College. Hampshire
